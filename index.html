Built for agencies

One vault for every client you serve.

From onboarding to automation, Passwordn keeps every credential organized, encrypted, and one click away — no matter which client you're working in.

Client groups

Every credential belongs to a client. Switch contexts instantly — see only Acme Corp's logins, or Harbor Digital's, without mixing accounts.

Zero-knowledge vault

Master password never leaves your device. AES-256-GCM encryption with 600k PBKDF2 rounds. Even we can't read your data.

Chrome extension

Switch to any client's context and autofill their Squarespace, WordPress, GoDaddy, or n8n — without opening a new tab or copy-pasting.

Subscription tracker

See every recurring charge across all your clients and tools. Connect a card or upload a statement to catch forgotten subscriptions.

Service catalog

60+ pre-configured services — Webflow, Vercel, n8n, GitHub, Adobe, Stripe — with login URLs and direct links to billing management.

API & MCP

Expose your vault as a REST API or Claude MCP server. Hand credentials to n8n workflows and AI agents without ever showing the plaintext.
